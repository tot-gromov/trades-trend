**Trades-Trend.com** is Konstantin Gromov's blog about trading and investing.

**Focus of the site:**
- Training beginners from scratch: from basic concepts (“what is trading”) to choosing a broker and making your first deposit
- Practical strategies: scalping, money management, trading imbalances (FVG/iFVG)
- Cryptocurrencies: guides to Bitcoin, types of crypto (altcoins, stablecoins, DeFi, memecoins)
- Algotrading and neural networks in trading
- Psychology: how not to lose your deposit, dealing with tilt and emotions

**Features:**
- Live signals with delayed broadcast and hit statistics
- Focus on the Binarium platform and binary options
- Comparative market reviews (Forex, crypto, stock market)
- Content in two languages (Russian/English)

**Target audience:** novice traders who want to understand the markets and start trading with a minimum deposit ($10–100).
